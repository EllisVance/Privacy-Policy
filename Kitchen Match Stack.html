Kitchen Match Stack Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Kitchen Match Stack is designed as an offline casual puzzle game.

Accounts

Kitchen Match Stack does not require an account, sign-in, or profile creation.

Personal Data

Kitchen Match Stack does not collect personal data and does not upload personal data from the device.

Local Storage

The game stores progress and preferences locally on the device. Local data may include current level, unlocked level, coins, power-up counts, sound and vibration settings, selected background, and daily bonus claim date.

Network Services

The game does not use a backend service for gameplay. It does not use Firebase and does not use Google Play Services.

Advertising and Tracking

Kitchen Match Stack does not include ads and does not use advertising tracking or third-party tracking SDKs.

Data Sharing

Because the game does not collect personal data, it does not sell or share personal data.

Deleting Local Data

Players can reset progress in the game settings or remove the app from the device to delete locally stored game data.
